The Ongoing Debate of Article 9

What is Article 9 and why is it debated?

Website's Purpose

If you want to talk about Japanese politics, you simply cannot avoid the hot topic of Article 9. It is a difficult topic to understand and discuss, yet the importance of being able to do so is great. This website introduces Article 9 to those who don't know about it at all, and gives an introduction to the reasons behind its hot debate and its importance.
